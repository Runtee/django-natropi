At Natropi, we offer flexible and tailored loan solutions to meet your financial needs. Whether you're seeking personal or business financing, we've got you covered with two primary loan categories based on the loan amount and eligibility criteria.

Loan Term 1

For loan amounts from $50,000 to $500,000

  • Interest Rate: 5.99% - 6.99% per annum
  • Repayment Term: 12 to 60 months
  • Repayment Frequency: Monthly
  • Origination Fee: 2% - 5% of loan amount, paid upfront
  • Late Payment Fee: 2% of outstanding balance or $225, whichever is greater
  • Prepayment Penalty: None
  • Default Interest Rate: 18% per annum
  • Collateral: None (unsecured loan)
  • Credit Score Requirement: 600 or above
  • Income Requirement: $100,000 per annum (personal) or $250,000 (business)
  • Debt-to-Income Ratio: 40% or less

Loan Term 2

For loan amounts of $1,000,000 or more

  • Interest Rate: 3.99% - 4.99% per annum
  • Repayment Term: 24 to 84 months
  • Repayment Frequency: Monthly
  • Origination Fee: 1.5% - 3% of loan amount, paid upfront
  • Late Payment Fee: 1.5% of outstanding balance or $550, whichever is greater
  • Prepayment Penalty: None
  • Default Interest Rate: 15% per annum
  • Collateral: May require collateral (case-by-case)
  • Credit Score Requirement: 650 or above
  • Income Requirement: $500,000 per annum (personal) or $1,000,000 (business)
  • Debt-to-Income Ratio: 30% or less

How to Apply for a Loan

To get started with your loan application:

  1. Ensure that you have made a deposit to the app prior to applying.
  2. Fill out our loan application form and submit the required documentation.
  3. We will evaluate your application and determine the best terms for you.
  4. Upon approval, you will receive your loan, and repayment will begin based on the agreed terms.
